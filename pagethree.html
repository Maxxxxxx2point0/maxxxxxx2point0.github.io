What is Randomness?

Randomness is a bunch of objects or things that
are selected without reason. The following things are randomness
in list form:

  • Banana
  • green
  • copper
  • zinc
  • petroleum
  • laptop.
    • A laptop has components made out of two
      of the items in the previous list
    • Copper and petroleum are the two components
