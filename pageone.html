Christianity

Christianity is a religion, a faith-based way of life. Numerous amounts of people around the world follow this religion.
Some are rocks in it, others are just beginning in their journey, some might even be a follower for years, but still are not
fully sold in the idea of there being a living God controlling everything. I am one of those people who are not completely sold on that idea,
but I am slowly becoming a rock in the religion. Some people might be asking God why or what next? The truth is these questions,
no one knows. We have no insight as to what will happen in the future, Jesus might not even know because of the Trinity.
God is the all-knowing, and He decides to let others know what the plan is. Yet, people are still drawn to God.
This is because they long to know that there is something out there that created everything and knows what will happen.
They want to know that they are loved and were created for a purpose. That is why Christianity is a faith-based way of life.
Not just a way of life. No one knows what will happen until it happens.
