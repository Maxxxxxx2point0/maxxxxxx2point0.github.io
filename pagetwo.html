Box or Pouch?

A juice box is juice in a box. Juice is juice in a glass. Why on earth would anybody make juice in a pouch. They fall over way easier, make more messes when they are opened, and use stupid flimsy straws to poke in a tiny little hole.

Juice: Something from fruits?

Juice is something that fruit has in it.
Fruit is filled with water and the flavoring for the water is chemicals that the fruit produces.
When fruit is squished it creates a liquid that is called juice.
